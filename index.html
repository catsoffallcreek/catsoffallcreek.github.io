Hi, & welcome to the website for Cats of Fall Creek!

After walking around taking cat photos for months, we've made a new calendar for 2021! In the process, we've enjoyed meeting and chatting with many of the cat owners around the neighborhood. This year's calendar has sixteen (16(!)) cats in it, some of which are the nicest/cutest/fluffiest cats we've ever met. It also features the photo contest winners—we're excited to share them with you!

If you have any questions, just email us at catsoffallcreek@gmail.com. Remember to like us on facebook or myspace so more people learn about the calendars!

Celebrate the cats!
Ben & Greg
